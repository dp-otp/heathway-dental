Privacy Policy

Data Controller: The Heathway Dental Surgery, 276a Heathway, Dagenham, Essex, RM10 8QS

What we collect: Name, contact details, appointment information, and health records necessary for dental treatment.

Why we collect it: To provide dental care, manage appointments, send reminders, and comply with NHS/CQC regulations.

How we store it: Data is stored securely using encrypted cloud systems (Firebase). Access is restricted to authorised practice staff only.

Data retention: Patient records are kept for 10 years after last treatment (or until age 25 for children), in line with NHS guidelines.

Your rights: You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Contact us at 020 8592 5030 or visit the practice.

Cookies: We use essential cookies for site functionality. No third-party tracking cookies are used without your consent.

For concerns about data handling, contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

Complaints Procedure

We are committed to providing the highest standard of dental care. If you are unhappy with any aspect of our service, we want to hear from you.

Step 1: Speak to your dentist or our practice manager directly. Most concerns can be resolved immediately.

Step 2: If unresolved, write to: Practice Manager, The Heathway Dental Surgery, 276a Heathway, Dagenham, Essex, RM10 8QS. We will acknowledge within 3 working days and respond within 10 working days.

Step 3 — NHS patients: Contact NHS England at england.nhs.uk/contact-us/complaint or call 0300 311 22 33.

Step 3 — Private patients: Contact the Dental Complaints Service at dcs.org.uk or call 020 8253 0800.

GDC: You may also contact the General Dental Council at gdc-uk.org if you believe a dental professional's fitness to practise is impaired.
