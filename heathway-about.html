About Us

Our Story

Caring for Dagenham's smiles since 2003

The Heathway Dental Surgery has been serving the Dagenham community for over two decades. Located at 276a Heathway, Dagenham, Essex RM10 8QS, we are conveniently situated near Dagenham Heathway station, making quality dental care easily accessible for local residents.

Our practice has a proud history of providing dental care to the area. Originally established by Mr Omran Abbas, the practice later became known as Kaushal Abbas before being taken over by Dr Niti Kaushal, who continues to lead the surgery as Principal Dentist and Owner.

Our Team

Led by Dr Niti Kaushal

Dr Niti Kaushal is the Principal Dentist and Owner of The Heathway Dental Surgery. With a commitment to providing the highest standard of dental care, Dr Kaushal leads an experienced and friendly team dedicated to making every patient feel comfortable and confident in their treatment.

From our welcoming reception team to our skilled dental professionals, every member of staff at Heathway Dental is passionate about patient care. We understand that visiting the dentist can sometimes feel daunting, and we go above and beyond to create a calm, supportive environment for patients of all ages.

  • Experienced, qualified dental professionals
  • Warm, welcoming reception team
  • Dedicated to patient comfort and care
  • Treating adults and children alike
Our Practice

A modern, welcoming environment

Our practice features comfortable waiting areas and well-equipped treatment rooms designed to provide you with the best possible dental experience. We maintain the highest standards of hygiene and infection control, ensuring a safe environment for every visit.

As both an NHS and private practice, we offer a comprehensive range of treatments to suit every patient's needs and budget. Whether you require routine check-ups and preventive care or more advanced dental treatments, we have the facilities and expertise to help.

  • Modern, well-equipped treatment rooms
  • Comfortable, relaxing waiting area
  • Highest hygiene and safety standards
  • Full range of NHS and private treatments
